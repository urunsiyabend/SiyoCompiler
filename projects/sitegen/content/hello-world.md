# Welcome to Siyo Site Generator

This is the **first post** generated by the Siyo static site generator.

## Features

The generator supports *italic text*, **bold text**, and `inline code`.

You can also add [links](https://github.com) to your posts.

### How It Works

Write your content in Markdown files inside the `content/` directory. The generator parses front matter, converts Markdown to HTML, and applies templates.

Simple and fast.
